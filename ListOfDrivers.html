Drivers

You can't use Appium without at least one driver!.

Note: Appium version 1 most of the drivers are installed automatically. Here are the drivers that are currently maintained by the Appium team.

Sno Driver Platform(s) Mode(s)
1 Chromium macOS, Windows, Linux Web
2 Espresso Android Native
3 Gecko macOS, Windows, Linux, Android Web
4 Mac2 macOS Native
5 Safari macOS, iOS Web
6 UiAutomator2 Android Native, Hybrid, Web
7 XCUITest iOS Native, Hybrid, Web
